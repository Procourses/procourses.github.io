About

Academic Profile

Rizwan Mushtaq is an Associate Professor of Finance and Data Science at EDC Paris Business School. He earned his PhD in Financial Economics from the University of Paris I: Panthéon-Sorbonne and holds an MS in Banking & Finance.

His research focuses on Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), sustainable finance and governance, financial inclusion, and applications of AI and machine learning in finance. He heads the MSc Data Science & Business Analytics programme.

He has published in leading journals including the Journal of Business Ethics, Journal of Business Research, International Journal of Finance & Economics, and Applied Economics.

Academic Research

Research Overview

Advancing knowledge at the intersection of corporate governance, sustainability, financial inclusion, and AI in finance.

Focus Areas

Research Themes

Corporate Governance

Board composition, CEO power, gender diversity, and their effects on CSR reporting and environmental decoupling.

CSR & Sustainability

CSR disclosure, environmental performance, sustainability committees, and ESG–financial outcome linkages.

Financial Inclusion & ICT

Microfinance, ICT adoption, SME access to finance, and the impact of financial inclusion on poverty and growth.

AI & ML in Finance

Machine learning for financial markets, NLP on 10-K filings, AI-driven predictions and green bond analysis.
