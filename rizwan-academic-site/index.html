About

Academic Profile

Rizwan Mushtaq is an Associate Professor of Finance and Data Science at EDC Paris Business School. He earned his PhD in Financial Economics from the University of Paris I: Panthéon-Sorbonne. He also holds an MS in Banking & Finance.

His research focuses on Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), sustainable finance and governance, financial inclusion, and the applications of AI and machine learning in finance and economics. He is responsible for the Data Science & Business Intelligence specialisation within the Grande École Programme.

Prof. Mushtaq has published extensively in leading international journals including the Journal of Business Ethics, Journal of Business Research, International Journal of Finance & Economics, Review of Quantitative Finance & Accounting, and Applied Economics.
